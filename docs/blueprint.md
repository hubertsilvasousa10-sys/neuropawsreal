# **App Name**: Neuropaws Calming Journey

## Core Features:

- Quiz for Personalization: A sequence of questions to understand the dog's anxiety and owner's dedication levels, capturing name and email.
- Personalized Recommendations: Based on the quiz answers, the system recommends appropriate solutions from the product offerings.
- Video Sales Letter (VSL) Integration: Embed a 9:16 video explaining the benefits of the Neuropaws method.
- Benefits Display: Showcase key benefits: reduced anxiety, improved sleep, reactivity control, decreased object destruction, and stronger bonding.
- Bonus Presentation: Highlight exclusive bonuses like a daily checklist, vagus nerve activation exercises, and calming sounds playlist.
- Testimonials Section: Display real testimonials and reviews from satisfied customers.
- FAQ Section: Comprehensive FAQ answering at least 11 common questions about the method, addressing concerns and objections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#8EC9FF), inspired by the prompt, evokes a feeling of calm and relaxation.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E5F3FF) creates a soft and airy feel.
- Accent color: Slightly darker blue (#6CA0DC) will highlight important sections of the sales page and will create a visual interest.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its clarity and legibility, will present a contemporary style, perfect for web content.
- Use simple, line-based icons related to calmness, dogs, and well-being.
- Clean and spacious layout with clear section headings and visual breaks.
- Subtle fade-in animations as the user scrolls through the page.
